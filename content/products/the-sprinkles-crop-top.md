The Sprinkles Crop Top is queer-owned NYC apparel for the gay men who travel in flocks. "Sprinkles," plural, because the dance floor is always more fun when at least three of you are wearing matching shirts and pretending it wasn't on purpose. We made this for the friend groups whose roof party is the destination, whose Cherry Grove weekend is annual, and whose Pride march outfit was coordinated in a Notes app three weeks in advance.

The body is a soft, lightweight cotton blend cropped at the natural waist with a clean raw-hem-adjacent finish that doesn't curl after the first wash. True unisex sizing, runs cropped by design. Size down for a tighter silhouette, stay true to size for an inch-and-a-half of midriff above your high-waisted shorts. The graphic is centered, oversized, and scaled to read across the rooftop. Drop sleeves hang the way they're supposed to. Shoulder seams are reinforced.

The Sprinkles Tank Top is the queer-owned NYC piece for the gay men who travel in flocks and refuse to do summer alone. "Sprinkles," plural, because the dance floor doesn't fully turn on until at least three of you are matching, and the volleyball court at Christopher Street Pier doesn't really start until your group rolls up wearing the same tank in different prints from the BLA core line.

The fabric is a soft, breathable poly-cotton blend with the slight tonal slub of our standard tank base. Pre-washed for honest second-cycle measurements. Armholes run deep — the kind that show the side bicep work you've been doing since March — and the shoulders are reinforced with double-stitched seams. True unisex sizing; runs slightly fitted. Size up for a more relaxed drape, stay true for a closer silhouette over high-waisted shorts. The graphic is centered, oversized, and scaled to read across a Pride dance floor or the Sunday Tea pavilion at Cherry Grove.

Wear this gay statement tank with the rest of your friend group wearing the rest of the Sprinkles line and let the coordinated photos take care of themselves. Wear it on a Fire Island weekend, on a Bushwick warehouse rooftop, on the L train at noon, on the Pier on a 92-degree afternoon, on a Sunday morning brunch where everyone is in the previous night's outfit and nobody is willing to admit it. The Sprinkles Tank Top works for the chosen-family chaos that defines NYC gay summer.

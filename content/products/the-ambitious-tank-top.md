The Ambitious Tank Top is queer-owned NYC apparel for the gay men whose summer schedules look like a Google Calendar with no breaks between gym, brunch, beach, dance floor, and walk of shame. We made this for the boys who refuse to take a rest day on visibility. The silhouette is racerback-adjacent, shoulders fully exposed, armholes deep enough to honor the work you've put in but not so deep that the drape falls apart on the dance floor. This is a working tank, not a costume.

Built on a soft, breathable poly-cotton blend with a slight stretch — light enough for August humidity, structured enough not to cling weirdly when sweat happens. True unisex sizing, runs slightly fitted; size up if you want a relaxed drape over high-waisted shorts. The graphic is centered front, oversized, and clean enough to read from the volleyball court at Christopher Street Pier or the dancefloor at a Bushwick warehouse party. The shoulder seams hold up to gym bags, beach bags, and being thrown over the back of a chair at 4am.

Wear this gay statement tank to the gym you joined to flirt at. Wear it to Fire Island. Wear it on a Pride float, on the L train, in line for an iced coffee, on the Christopher Street pier, at the Eagle, in your kitchen at 11am on a Sunday when nobody else is awake yet. The Ambitious Tank Top works for everyone with somewhere to be — which, if you're shopping at a queer-owned NYC apparel brand, is probably everyone.

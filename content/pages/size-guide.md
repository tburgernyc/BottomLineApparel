Print-on-demand means we can't do sizing exchanges, so this page matters. Measurements below are the manufacturer's specs (mostly Bella+Canvas, with a few Independent Trading Co. and AS Colour pieces). When in doubt, measure a tee you already love and own — chest width pit-to-pit, length collar-to-hem — and match it.

All measurements are in inches.

## Unisex Tees

Our standard tee is the Bella+Canvas 3001, soft cotton, slightly fitted unisex cut. Order your usual tee size — if you wear a Medium in everything else, wear a Medium here. Pre-shrunk; expect minimal shrinkage if you wash cold and tumble low.

| Size | Chest (width) | Length |
| --- | --- | --- |
| S | 18 | 28 |
| M | 20 | 29 |
| L | 22 | 30 |
| XL | 24 | 31 |
| 2XL | 26 | 32 |

## Crop Hoodies

These run cropped. That's the point. The body length sits above the natural waist — please look at the table, not your gut. If you usually wear a Medium hoodie, the crop hoodie in Medium will hit roughly mid-rib. Order up one size if you want it to land lower.

| Size | Chest (width) | Length |
| --- | --- | --- |
| S | 19 | 19 |
| M | 21 | 20 |
| L | 23 | 21 |
| XL | 25 | 22 |

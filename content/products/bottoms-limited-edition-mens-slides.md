Slip on, walk out, deal with whatever the day looks like once you've already left the apartment. We made these for the boys who treat the trip from the front door to the bodega like a fashion moment but want to keep it under three minutes of effort. Cushioned, lightweight, and broken in from the moment they arrive.

The footbed is a cushioned EVA molded for arch support that doesn't punish your feet on a long Brooklyn block walk. True men's sizing — order your normal slide size; check the size guide if you're between, because the strap runs slightly narrower than a Birkenstock.

The Loose Tank Top is the queer-owned NYC piece for the gay men who refuse to commit to a fitted silhouette in August. Loose through the body, deep at the armhole, soft at the shoulder — this is the tank you reach for when you want the air conditioning to actually do its job. We cut it generously so it skims rather than clings, dropping just past the high-waisted-shorts line in a way that drapes and moves with you, not against you.

The fabric is a soft, breathable cotton blend with the slight tonal texture of our standard tank base. Pre-washed for honest second-cycle measurements. Armholes run deep (yes, the kind that show side bicep when you raise your arms), shoulder straps are relaxed, hem is finished clean. True unisex sizing; runs loose by design — size down if you want a closer drape, true to size for the intended relaxed shape. The graphic is large, centered front, scaled to read at a distance.

This gay statement tank works in the heat, on the dance floor, on the volleyball court, on the L train, and during any rooftop happy hour where the dress code is "summer." Layer over a swim suit on the way back from Fire Island. Throw on with the sweat shorts you bought to wear to the gym and have not, in fact, worn to the gym. Wear it to the bagel shop. Wear it in a movie theater with strong AC. The Loose Tank Top is for the boys who already know that less fabric is more confidence.

The Origin

Gay NYC, Bad Decisions, Great Tees

Born on late-night trains and gay group chats, Bottom Line Apparel is what happens when a New York gay gets tired of straight brands trying to make "funny gay shirts."

We make innuendo-heavy tees and hoodies for millennial and Gen Z gay men who live for clout, chaos, and camp — but still want something they'd actually wear outside the bedroom.

From "Push Out For Clout" to "Gapin' and Vapin'," every design is a self-drag, an inside joke, or a love letter to the messiness of gay life in big cities. You bring the body. We bring the punchline.
